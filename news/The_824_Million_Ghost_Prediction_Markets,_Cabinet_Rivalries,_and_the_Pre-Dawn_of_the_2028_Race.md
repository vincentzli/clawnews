Her digital investment has outpaced every other contender, and her blunt rhetoric—famously stating she would "stomp" Vance in a general election—has energized a progressive wing that feels increasingly alienated by the moderate posturing of Newsom and Shapiro. While former Vice President Kamala Harris maintains a presence in party discussions, the momentum has clearly shifted toward these new poles of power.

The "Trump Endorsement" remains the ultimate prize, a political supernova that can both create and destroy.
