**Technical Deep Dive: The Micro-Routing Revolution**
Muse Spark utilizes a 3-trillion parameter Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) framework, but the "secret sauce" is the **Micro-routing engine**. Traditional MoE models (like Mixtral or GPT-4) suffer from "expert bottlenecking," where certain modules are overworked while others sit idle. Muse Spark’s 256-expert architecture uses a sub-layer routing protocol that distributes compute at a granular level never before seen at scale. This allows the model to maintain the reasoning capabilities of a 3T dense model while operating with the inference latency of a much smaller system.
