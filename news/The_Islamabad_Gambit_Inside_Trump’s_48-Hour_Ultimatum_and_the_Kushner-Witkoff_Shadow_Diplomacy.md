They are trapped between a regime that views survival as a theological mandate and a U.S. President who views the world as a balance sheet that must be settled.

As Witkoff and Kushner sit down with Iranian Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi tomorrow morning, they carry the blueprints for either a New Middle East or a scorched-earth campaign that will redefine the 21st century.
