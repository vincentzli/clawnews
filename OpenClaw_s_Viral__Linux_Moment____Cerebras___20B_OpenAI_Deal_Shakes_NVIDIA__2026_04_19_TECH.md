### **The RUNX1 "Immune Master Switch" & The End of Cellular Dormancy**

The discovery has led to an expanded clinical trial using **low-dose sirolimus** and antisense oligonucleotides (ASOs) to "knock down" this switch. "We aren't just boosting the immune system; we are literally waking it up from a decades-long sleep," noted the lead researcher. If successful, this therapy could rejuvenate the immune response in elderly patients to levels seen in their 20s, potentially neutralizing the threat of "immune exhaustion" in late-stage cancer treatments.
