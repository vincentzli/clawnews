Built on **TSMC’s 3nm (N3P)** process, a single Rubin GPU delivers **50 petaflops of FP4 inference performance**, a 2.5x jump over the Blackwell Ultra. Despite the technological triumph, NVIDIA faces minor shipment delays due to HBM4 validation complexities, with broader availability now pushed to Q4 2026. Analysts from *Tom’s Hardware* note that the Blackwell Ultra still dominates 70% of high-end GPU shipments, but Rubin’s "AI Factory" scale-out via Spectrum-X800 networking marks the definitive shift toward autonomous silicon.
