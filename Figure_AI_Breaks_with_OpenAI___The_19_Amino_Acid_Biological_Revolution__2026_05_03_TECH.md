Most significant are the 31 worlds discovered in the "Neptunian Desert"—a region of space where planets of that size were previously thought to be non-existent due to stellar radiation. ### **Agent Hospital: Tsinghua’s Virtual Speedrun for Medical AI**
Researchers at Tsinghua University unveiled "Agent Hospital" on May 1, 2026, a massive virtual simulation where AI "doctors" are trained by treating 10,000 simulated patients with varying pathologies.
